No Name
  • Editorial Reviews
    • Jack White announced the official release of his acclaimed new album, No Name, following its clandestine white-label appearance at Third Man Records locations where customers were slipped, guerilla-style, free unmarked vinyl copies in their shopping bags. True to his DIY roots, this album was recorded, produced, and mixed by White at his Third Man Studio throughout 2023 and 2024, pressed to vinyl at Third Man Pressing, and released by Third Man Records.

Div-idy's Recommendation

Based on the diverse range of reviews, it seems that opinions on this album vary among listeners. While some praise it for its energy and return to a familiar sound, others feel it falls short of White's previous works. If you are a fan of Jack White's music, particularly his White Stripes era, you may find this album enjoyable. It's recommended to give it a listen and form your own opinion.
