Div-idy's Recommendation

This extended, 2 disc version of Neil Diamond's All-Time Greatest Hits is recommended for fans and music enthusiasts who appreciate a comprehensive collection of his memorable songs. The set offers good value for the number of tracks included, providing a mix of classic hits and additional, lesser-known gems. While some older recordings may not be as well remastered, overall, the positive feedback and satisfaction from customers make it a worthwhile purchase for Neil Diamond admirers.
