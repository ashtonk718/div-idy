Fireworks & Rollerblades
  • Editorial Reviews
    • Benson Boone's intimate, diaristic pop songs are constantly evolving as he quickly becomes a global hitmaker. His latest single, the smash hit "Beautiful Things," quickly soared into the Top 10 of the Billboard Hot 100 after topping charts across the globe such as #1 Billboard Global 200 and #1 Billboard Global Excl. US for the 4th consecutive week in a row. Benson is reaching new heights in 2024, with over 2.5 billion career streams to date, an upcoming sold out world tour, and a hotly anticipated debut album Fireworks & Rollerblades on the way. This album includes not only unreleased music, but his previous hit songs such as his breakout single "GHOST TOWN", which was Boone's first entry on the Billboard Hot 100 and a Top 25 hit at both Top 40 and Hot AC radio and "In The Stars," which is now certified Platinum.
