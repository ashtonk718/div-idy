Pros & Cons

Pros Cons
1. Fast A13 Bionic chip for smooth performance 1. Some users may face issues with setting up Apple ID
2. Crisp and vibrant 10.2-inch Retina display 2. Lack of USB-C charging port
3. Long battery life with all-day usage 3. Limited storage options (64GB or 256GB)
4. Touch ID for secure unlocking and payments 4. Lack of cellular data option in some models
5. Front and back cameras with Center Stage feature 5. Lightning charging port instead of USB-C
6. Affordable price for the features offered

Div-idy's Recommendation

Considering the fast performance, vibrant display, long battery life, secure Touch ID, and affordable price, the iPad (9th Generation) with A13 Bionic chip is a great choice for users looking for a versatile and capable tablet. However, potential buyers should be aware of the limitations in storage options and the need for additional accessories due to the lack of certain ports.
