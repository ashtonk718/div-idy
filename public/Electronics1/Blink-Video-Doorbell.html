Pros & Cons

Pros Cons
1. Easy installation 1. Mount may feel less solid and rock a bit
2. Works well with existing smart home components and chimes 2. Audio quality issues reported by some users
3. No monthly subscription fees for legacy Blink customers 3. Motion detection settings may not work perfectly in all scenarios
4. Long-lasting battery life and easy battery replacement 4. Protrudes more from the wall compared to other doorbells
5. Reasonably priced cloud storage subscription 5. Instructions may lack details about certain product features
6. High-quality video and two-way audio feature

Div-idy's Recommendation

Overall, the Blink Video Doorbell offers a great mix of features with easy installation, compatibility with existing smart home setups, and no monthly subscription fees for legacy Blink customers. However, some users have reported audio quality issues and minor concerns with motion detection settings. If these cons are not major concerns for you, the Blink Video Doorbell could be a reliable and cost-effective choice for your home security needs.
