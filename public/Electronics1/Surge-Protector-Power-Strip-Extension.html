6 Ft Surge Protector Power Strip - 8 Widely Outlets with 4 USB Ports, 3 Side Outlet Extender with 6 Feet Extension Cord, Flat Plug, Wall Mount, Desk USB Charging Station, ETL,White
  • About this item

    The power strip features 4 AC outlets on each side, totaling 8 outlets along with 4 USB charging ports. The USB A ports offer a maximum output of 5V/2.4A each, while the USB C port supports 5V/3A. This surge protector can power up to 12 devices simultaneously and is compatible with a wide range of voltages from 120v to 240v circuit.

  • 3 Side Design & Wide Space

    The power strip's 3-side design ensures that none of the outlets are obstructed, offering greater convenience. With the 8 AC outlets spaced 1.8 inches apart, larger than the standard 1.5-inch socket spacing, it accommodates various devices easily. The compact design saves space, suitable for home, office, and college dorm rooms.

  • Multi Safety Protection

    Certified by RoHS, FCC, and ETL, this power strip provides comprehensive safety features including overload, short-circuit, over current, over-voltage, and overheating protection. With a minimum energy-absorbing capacity of 600 Joules, it safeguards your devices from power surges. The surge protector automatically cuts power to protect connected devices in case of overwhelming voltage surge.

  • 6 Ft Extension Cord with Flat Plug and Cable Ties

    The 6-feet right-angled flat plug power cord enhances flexibility in placement. Reusable fastening cable ties help in organizing and managing excess cord length efficiently. Additionally, the power strip features two mounting holes at the back for secure installation across various settings.

  • Our After Sale Service

    Backed by RoHS, FCC, and ETL certifications, our customer service team offers responsive assistance within 24 hours. Purchase with confidence, as we provide a 30-day return policy and a 12-month replacement warranty for your convenience.
