  • Bloomsbury presents Crown of Midnight by Sarah J. Maas, read by Elizabeth Evans.Never trust an assassin.Celaena’s story continues in this second book in the #1 bestselling Throne of Glass series by Sarah J. Maas.Celaena Sardothien won a brutal contest to become the King’s Champion. But she is far from loyal to the crown. Though she goes to great lengths to hide her secret, her deadly charade becomes more difficult when she realizes she is not the only one seeking justice. Her search for answers ensnares those closest to her, and no one is safe from suspicion—not the Crown Prince Dorian; not Chaol, the Captain of the Guard; not even her best friend, Nehemia, a princess with a rebel heart.Then, one terrible night, the secrets they have all been keeping lead to an unspeakable tragedy. As Celaena’s world shatters, she will be forced to decide once and for all where her true loyalties lie...and what she is willing to fight for.The second book in the #1 New York Times bestselling Throne of Glass series returns listeners to a land destroyed by liars, where one woman’s truth is the only thing that can save them all. Pros & Cons

Pros Cons
Well-written and engaging plot. Potential dependency on reading the preceding book.
Strong character development with depth and growth. Complexity in character relationships may be challenging for some readers.
Surprising plot twists that keep readers engaged. Need to pay close attention to details for full comprehension.
Richly crafted world-building and mythology. Possibility of overwhelming amount of characters to keep track of.
Compelling romance elements woven into the story. Romantic aspects may not appeal to all readers.
Action-packed sequences and intense moments. Certain scenes may be too graphic for sensitive readers.

Div-idy's Recommendation

The Throne of Glass series, specifically "Crown of Midnight" by Sarah J. Maas, presents a captivating and intricately woven story filled with engaging plotlines, well-developed characters, and unexpected twists. The rich world-building and compelling romance elements add depth to the narrative, creating a truly immersive reading experience. While the series may require commitment to fully appreciate its complexity, the action-packed sequences and intense moments make it a worthwhile journey for fans of fantasy fiction.
