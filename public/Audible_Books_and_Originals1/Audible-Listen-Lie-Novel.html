  • After Lucy is found wandering the streets, covered in her best friend Savvy’s blood, everyone thinks she is a murderer. Lucy and Savvy were the golden girls of their small Texas town: pretty, smart, and enviable. Lucy married a dream guy with a big ring and an even bigger new home. Savvy was the social butterfly loved by all, and if you believe the rumors, especially popular with the men in town. It’s been years since that horrible night, a night Lucy can’t remember anything about, and she has since moved to LA and started a new life.
  • But now the phenomenally huge hit true crime podcast "Listen for the Lie," and its too-good looking host Ben Owens, have decided to investigate Savvy’s murder for the show’s second season. Lucy is forced to return to the place she vowed never to set foot in again to solve her friend’s murder, even if she is the one that did it.

Pros & Cons

Pros Cons
Captivating thriller with twists and turns. Slow pacing and predictable storyline for some readers.
Compelling main character with strong personality. Opportunity missed for more romance/tension in the plot.
Engaging writing style and humorous moments. Some readers may find the ending prolonged and lacking closure.
Intriguing dual timelines and multiple points of view. Limited character depth in certain areas.
Well-developed scandal and drama elements. Minimal exploration of certain plot points and character backstories.

Div-idy's Recommendation

"Listen for the Lie" by Amy Tintera offers a captivating and engaging thriller experience with its intriguing storyline, compelling characters, and well-executed twists. Fans of true-crime podcasts and mysteries will likely enjoy the suspenseful narrative and the unique perspective of a protagonist struggling with memory loss amidst suspicions of murder. While some readers may find certain aspects predictable or wish for more depth in character development, the overall entertainment value and suspenseful atmosphere make this book a recommended read for those seeking a thrilling and dramatic story."
