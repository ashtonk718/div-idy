Div-idy's Recommendation

The book seems to offer a compelling and engaging reading experience with its well-developed characters, intriguing plot twists, and fast-paced writing style. While some readers may find the character's moral ambiguity a downside, overall, the book provides a good balance of suspense and character development. It may require some patience at the beginning, but once the story takes off, readers are captivated by the narrative. For those who enjoy a mix of mystery, suspense, and well-crafted characters, this book is likely to be a satisfying read.
